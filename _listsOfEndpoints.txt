Other notes

The frontend directory is mounted at / via StaticFiles(html=True) (so static/index routes are served by StaticFiles rather than decorated handlers).
Path parameters in routes: {job_id}, {execution_id}, {workflow_id} — treat as dynamic segments.
There are a few duplicated/near-duplicate decorator entries (e.g., /api/functions/cataloging/start appears more than once in the source — meaning there may be multiple registrations or re-definitions to review).
